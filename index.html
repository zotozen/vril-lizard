What are Vril Lizards?

Vril Lizards are described in conspiracy circles, most notably by Donald Marshall, as a sinister, reptilian species living beneath the Earth. According to these claims, Vril are ancient, parasitic beings capable of taking over human hosts through a process called drone bodysnatching, where their consciousness and control can replace a person's own mind.

  • Marshall alleges the Vril have made secret pacts with global elites, particularly royalty, powerful politicians, and media moguls.
  • This theory suggests that Vril lizards help enable world domination agendas, in return for resources and secrecy.
  • The Vril takeover process, sometimes called "droning," supposedly involves a parasitic invasion through the human eye, permanently erasing the victim's consciousness.

Theories at a Glance

  1. Ancient Origins: Marshall links the Vril to legends like Atlantis and esoteric books (e.g., Bulwer-Lytton’s "Vril: The Power of the Coming Race"), claiming Nazis sought their lost technology through occult practices.
  2. Pacts with Power: Secret alliances, he says, date back centuries, with world leaders trading human lives for hidden riches and otherworldly knowledge.
  3. Bodysnatching: Instead of obvious invasion, Vril infiltration is framed as a “body snatcher” scenario. High-status individuals can be replaced with Vril-possessed “drones.”
  4. Cloning Centers: Marshall alleges elites, including celebrities and politicians, gather in secret “REM-driven cloning centers” to witness Vril bodysnatching rituals and maintain compliance.
